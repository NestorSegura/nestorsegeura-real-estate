   - Update `POST_BY_SLUG_QUERY`: add to BOTH coalesce branches: `_updatedAt`, `category`, `tags`, `"estimatedReadingTime": round(length(pt::text(body)) / 5 / 180)`, change `author` to `"author": author->{ name, bio, image }`

   - Use `defineQuery` from `next-sanity` for all new queries (not `groq`)
